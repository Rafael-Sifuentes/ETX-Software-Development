Our Expertise

What we do.

Custom website development.

Every website we build is coded from the ground up — no drag-and-drop builders, no bloated templates. Whether it's a single landing page or a fully featured site, we build it to be fast, scalable, and built to last.

  • Responsive & Mobile Friendly

    Mobile is the default experience for most people. We make sure your site looks and functions great across all screen sizes. No pinching, zooming, or guesswork required.

  • Search Engine Optimization

    Good SEO starts with clean code and a solid structure. We build your site to be searchable, indexable, and readable by real people and search engines alike.

  • Domain & Hosting Setup

    Don't want to deal with GoDaddy or DNS settings? We'll take care of domain setup, DNS configuration, and get your hosting environment ready so you can focus on your business.

  • Business Email Setup

    Get a custom email address (like you@yourcompany.com) set up to match your website domain. It's a small touch that goes a long way in professionalism.

  • QR Codes

    Need a smart way to connect your print materials to your website? We can generate QR codes that link to menus, contact pages, or custom landing pages — designed and tested to scan quickly and clearly.

  • Drone Photography

    Need high-quality aerial photos for your website? We offer drone photography services to showcase properties, projects, or storefronts from above.

  • Logo clean up

    Already have a logo, but it's low-res or not web-ready? We can clean it up and prepare formats optimized for digital use.

  • Google business profile management

    Boost your local visibility with Google Business Profile optimization. We help you connect with customers searching for your services on Google Maps and local search results.

  • Scheduling Software

    Streamline your operations with our scheduling software solution. Easily manage appointments, availability, and bookings online to improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.

About us

Who we are.

ETX Software is a web development studio based in Nacogdoches Texas. We help contractors, service providers, restaurants, and real estate professionals get fast, clean, and purpose-built websites that are custom made to suit their business and their customers.

  • 1.

    Plan

    We start with a conversation to understand your goals, content, and customer base. From there, we build a clear plan for your website structure and functionality.

  • 2.

    Design

    We craft a design tailored to your business, focused on clarity, ease of use, and mobile responsiveness. Every element is intentional.

  • 3.

    Build

    We hand-code your website from scratch using modern web development practices. No templates. No bloated frameworks. Just fast, clean code.

  • 4.

    Deploy

    Once approved, we launch your site with precision and care. We handle hosting setup, testing, performance optimization, and ensure your site is secure and ready for real-world use.

We're not a marketing agency, and we don't pretend to be. We believe that great development is its own craft. Our goal is simple: to help you launch a website that works as hard as you do.
