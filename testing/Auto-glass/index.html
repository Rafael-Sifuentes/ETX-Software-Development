    Some frequently asked questions

    • How long will it take ?

      Most chip repairs take around 30 minutes. Full windshield replacements usually take 60-90 minutes, plus additional curing time for the adhesive.

    • Can you replace glass on all makes and models ?

      Absolutely. We service cars, trucks, SUVs, and even some commercial vehicles. Just give us the year, make, and model when you book.

    • How soon can I drive my vehicle after a new windshield is installed ?

      For safety, we recommend waiting at least one hour after installation to allow the adhesive to cure properly—especially in hot or humid conditions.

    • Do you work with my insurance?

      At this time, we are not working with insurance providers due to their complexity and lengthy turn-around times.
