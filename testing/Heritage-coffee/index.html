All about us

Welcome to Heritage Coffee Co! Nestled in the heart of Lufkin, Texas, our faith-based, family-run coffee shop is your cozy haven for savoring handcrafted brews and homemade pastries. Step inside, and let the rich aroma of heritage, tradition, and a warm cup of joe fill your soul.

We use only the finest ethically sourced coffee beans, roasted to perfection to highlight their unique flavor profiles. We craft delectable pastries daily using fresh, high-quality ingredients, ensuring a delicious treat to complement your cup. Whether you're seeking a quiet corner to catch up with a friend, a vibrant space to fuel your workday, or a place to connect with like-minded individuals, Heritage Coffee Co. is your warm and welcoming escape.
